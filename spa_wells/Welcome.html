Welcome - Background

Will had always had a keen interest in music and after leaving education, he undertook an apprenticeship working for Tughan - Crane Music, Belfast. (sadly no longer trading)


Then thanks to his MUM persuading him he could do it  he completed a five year diploma course in Piano Tuning and Restoration organized by the Arts Council.....where incidentally he meet fellow enthusiast, graduate and life long friend Liam Gray!


Initially Will started his company Spa Wells Pianos  from his Dads garage then in 1981 Spa Wells opened shop premises in Ballynahinch and traded until 1996 when the business relocated to 120 Crossgar Road where he now enjoys the luxury of working from home!


Will has steadily built a reputation for quality and has a very loyal customer base both in the public and private sectors for over 30 years!
