Restoration Services

Will completed a 5 Year Diploma in Piano Tuning and Restoration at the Arts Council in 1981 and has been caring for pianos province wide ever since. Will is happy to undertake part or full restorations.

  5. Key Covering and Bushing

  6. Case work Repairs

  7. New Castors or Safety Castors Fitted

  8. Pedals, Trap-work fixed or Renewed

  9. Music Book Rest repaired or Replaced

  10. Woodworm Eradication
